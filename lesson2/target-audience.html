The Target Audience

The target audience for this website is New York residents who want to know the weather forecast daily.

Two Personas

Logan Williams

  • Age: 42
  • Education Level: Doctoral
  • Profession: Professor
  • Device: Desktop
  • Purpose: Give to his students weather forecast daily

CJ Thompson

  • Age: 36
  • Education Level: Master
  • Profession: Businessman
  • Device: Mobile
  • Purpose: His purpose is to calculate possible daily income based on Weather.

Five Scenarios

Scenario 1

Ross checks the weather every day to send Ben to the school with appropriate clothing.

Scenario 2

Janice wants to make a road trip this weekend and she's going to the beach. She needed to check the weather for the whole weekend.

Scenario 3

Chandler planned a romantic night in the mountains, he wanted to make sure that everything will be perfect to ask Monica to marry him.

Scenario 4

Joey has an important audition today, he checked the weather because he doesn't want to get there wet.

Scenario 5

Rachel and Phoebe have a goal of running in central park every day this month. They checked the weather to know if it will be possible.
